TEAM KONKI

This project is lead by Team KONKI which is a crew of four EPITA students. This project is part of our first year curriculum. Its four members are : Mathieu CAILLY (group leader), Rayan Sekkat, Alexandre Daood-Thompson and Robin Adjanohoun. We formed our group based on our friendship and common interest in video games.







Features


We have decided to create a video game. We think it could be an entertaining way to learn and we like the idea of achieving a project that we will be able to use afterwards.


Our game is called "Battle Car", it is a Third-Person-View (TPS) vehicle-based PC video-game. Its game-play is based on Player versus Player(PVP) combats in an on-line experience. Players will incarnate different types of vehicles and will be scattered around an open-world environment, where intense combats will take place. Following the game mode chosen, there will be different kinds of objectives. Cars will also feature aesthetics customization. We want players to begin all equals, regardless of their levels. The game focuses on an arcade based game-play, realism will stay at a basic level. The game will have a kind of reward system based on credits that players will obtain through playing.


Our game is called "Battle Car", it is a Third-Person-View (TPS) vehicle-based PC video-game. Its game-play is based on Player versus Player(PVP) combats in an on-line experience. Players will incarnate different types of vehicles and will be scattered around an open-world environment, where intense combats will take place. Following the game mode chosen, there will be different kinds of objectives. Cars will also feature aesthetics customization. We want players to begin all equals, regardless of their levels. The game focuses on an arcade based game-play, realism will stay at a basic level. The game will have a kind of reward system based on credits that players will obtain through playing.


The main game-play is vehicle-based combat, players will have to shoot enemies with a set of different weapons. Each vehicle will have a certain amount of health. Once the health is down to zero, the vehicle is destroyed. The elimination credit is given to the last player who downed the enemy player to zero. With a set of different type of weapons, we aim for diversity. The game will have different game mods, different maps, different weapons, and different type of cars.Enough content to provide a fun game-play.


The game lays on the concept that players all start with equal chances of winning. We do not want players to have from the start advantages upon others. Except from the type of vehicle, they will all start with nothing but the default weapon. It allows for an equitable game, and force people to adventure themselves through the map to obtain better gear.


The player incarnates a vehicle with a set of weapon. The vehicles can drive forward, backward, turn left and turn right, and brake. It also has a handbrake which allow for drifts. Every vehicle have a turbo which increase the speed for a limited amount of Nitrogen. The turbo will make the vehicle more difficult to handle for those few seconds. It will help for a diversity in the way people play the game. It allows for fast pushes through enemy territory and can serve as an escape when the situation requires it.


The player can take damages from enemy players, or from the environment whenever the shock with a player is too brutal. Driving in fire will also affect the player's health. As any multi-player games we want to let the player decide on how he is going to play our game. So we have to let him options to choose from, enough game-play style so every player can find his way to.







Game Mods

Team death match

Team death match has many similarities with the free-for-all mode but each player will be part of a team against an other one. The team with the biggest amount of point will win. And players will also earn credits regarding of its performances, if the player is part of the losing team, it will earn less credits.


Capture the flag

This game mode's objective is as its name suggests to capture a central flag localized around the middle of the map, and to bring it back in its base. Once captured, the flag will spawn back after a defined time. When the time expired, the game is over and the team with the most captured flag wins.


Biomes

The game will feature 2 (up to 3 if the schedule is respected). There will be 3 types of biomes : forest, snow, desert. It won't affect the player directly, but will only change the visual aesthetic of the game.


Obstacles

Each map will have different types of element such as buildings, vegetation, rocks, roads, and various kind of obstacles. They won't grant bonuses to players, but add some difficulty as colliding with one too fast will damage the vehicle. They also allow to set a layout on the map, to form a kind of arena.


Stations

Stations are little pods scattered around the map, where players can pass on them to obtain weapons. Once a player take the weapon of a station, it will stay disabled for a certain amount of time. Smaller stations which behave the same, will be more numerous on the map and will give players things such as ammunition, shields and Health.


Weapons


All weapons of Battle car can be picked up in-game through the stations. They all have the following stats : Damages, reloading speed, rate of fire, Range, Accuracy, Amount of max ammunition, amount of bullet in the magazine. They will all have their own ammunition which can be picked up on the ammunition stations.


Light Machine Gun

This is the default weapons, available with no ammunition constraint, it has a low caliber bullets and therefor does not inflict a lot of damages. It has a medium range. It serves the purpose to have at least one weapon when the player does not own one already or runs out of ammunition, so he has something to defend himself. Its accuracy will decrease with range.


Heavy Machine Gun

The Heavy Machine gun is a Weapon of high caliber, with a low rate of fire, it inflicts high damages at low-medium range. Its accuracy will decrease with range. This is a solid and consistent weapon.


Heavy Shotgun

The Heavy Shotgun is a weapon which shoots several projectiles in a cone shape. It inflicts huge damages at low range. Because of a low capacity magazine, players will need to reload more often than other weapons.


Sniper rifle

The Sniper rifle is a high range weapon, shooting high caliber bullet one at the time with a delay between each shot, dealing huge damages if the player success to hit its target.


Rocket Launcher

The Rocket launcher is a weapon which fires a rocket in a straight line in front of the player's vehicle. The rocket flies at a low speed, which lead to a longer travel time and impacts later. It inflicts high damages and has a very long range.


Mini Homing Rocket Launcher

The Mini Homing Rocket Launcher is a weapon which shoots several small rockets which target the enemy player and will try to hit it by flying to it. Each rockets inflicts a small amount of damages and won't have a long range.


Flamethrower

The Flamethrower is a close range weapon which spit a cone of fire in front of the player's vehicle, dealing damages the enemies in the cone, and setting them on fire dealing damages for a small amount of time.


Traps

The game will have different kind of traps such as mines. Which deals damages to enemies when triggered.
